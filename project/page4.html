Description and Images

Many battles occured in the Korean War but from the main 10 battles, you can see clearly who won. Other smaller battles such as the Battle of Osan, Operation Commando, and he Battle of White Horse also affected the war. The most important thing is that the battles were very close and all of them had severe casualties on both sides.
