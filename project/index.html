What is the Korean War

The Korean war was a devastating war that split Korea into North and South Korea. Korea is the only country remaining split.

Some things about the Korean War include:

  • Started in June 25, 1950
  • Ended in July 27, 1953
  • about 2.5 million total people died
  • about 1 million of them were korean

There is now a memorial for all the soldiers that participated in the Korean War in Washington D.C.

South Korea won the war but not without a cost. They had to rebuild up from nothing again. The 4 parts of this website include the history of the Korean War (aka what happened and how it happened), the aftermath (what was the result of the war, some fun facts about the Korean War, and images from the Korean War.
